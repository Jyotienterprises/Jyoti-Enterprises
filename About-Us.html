About Jyoti Enterprises

Driving innovation and excellence in industrial solutions since 1995

Our Story

Pioneers in Industrial Solutions

Founded in 1995 by Mr. Rajesh Sharma, Jyoti Enterprises began as a small supplier of industrial components in Mumbai. With a vision to provide quality products and exceptional service, we've grown into a leading distributor of industrial equipment across India.

Our journey started with just three employees and a 500 sq. ft. warehouse. Today, we operate from a state-of-the-art 50,000 sq. ft. facility with over 150 dedicated professionals serving more than 2,500 clients nationwide.

What sets us apart is our commitment to understanding our clients' unique requirements and providing tailored solutions that enhance their operational efficiency. We've built long-term relationships with industry leaders by consistently delivering value beyond expectations.

Our Journey

1995 - Foundation

Jyoti Enterprises founded with a focus on supplying industrial valves and fittings to local manufacturers in Mumbai.

2002 - First Expansion

Opened second warehouse in Pune and expanded product range to include pumps and compressors.

2008 - National Presence

Established regional offices in Delhi, Chennai, and Kolkata, becoming a pan-India supplier.

2015 - Technology Integration

Implemented ERP system and launched e-commerce platform to enhance customer experience.

2020 - New Facility

Inaugurated state-of-the-art 50,000 sq. ft. logistics and distribution center with automated systems.

2023 - Global Partnerships

Secured exclusive distribution rights for leading European and Japanese industrial brands.
