Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

What types of fasteners do you manufacture?

Jyoti Enterprises manufactures a wide range of fasteners including bolts, nuts, screws, washers, and special fasteners. Our product range includes standard fasteners as well as custom-designed fasteners for specific industrial applications. We use various materials including stainless steel, carbon steel, and alloy steel to meet different requirements.

Can you produce custom fasteners according to our specifications?

Yes, we specialize in custom fastener manufacturing. Our engineering team can work with your specifications to design and produce fasteners that meet your exact requirements, including unique dimensions, materials, threading, and head styles. We have the capability to create prototypes for testing before full-scale production.

What manufacturing capabilities do you have?

Our manufacturing facility includes state-of-the-art CNC machines, cold forging equipment, thread rolling machines, and heat treatment facilities. We have capabilities for both small batch production and high-volume manufacturing. Our quality control processes ensure that all fasteners meet specified standards and tolerances.

What materials do you work with?

We work with a variety of materials including:

  • Stainless Steel (SS 304, SS 316, etc.)
  • Carbon Steel (Grade 4.6, 8.8, 10.9, 12.9)
  • Alloy Steel
  • Brass
  • Aluminum
  • Special alloys upon request
We can also provide various surface treatments like zinc plating, hot dip galvanizing, and more.

What quality certifications do you hold?

Jyoti Enterprises is committed to quality excellence. We hold ISO 9001:2015 certification and our products meet international standards including DIN, ANSI, BS, and IS specifications. We have in-house testing facilities for dimensional checks, mechanical properties testing, and surface treatment verification.

How do you ensure product quality?

Our quality assurance process includes:

  • Raw material inspection
  • In-process quality checks at every production stage
  • Final inspection before packaging
  • Batch testing for mechanical properties
  • Traceability systems for all products
We also welcome third-party inspections and can provide material test certificates for all shipments.

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our standard MOQ is 1,000 pieces per item, but we can accommodate smaller quantities for sample orders or testing purposes. For custom fasteners, the MOQ may vary depending on the complexity of the product and tooling requirements. Please contact our sales team for specific MOQ information related to your needs.

What are your delivery times?

Standard products from our inventory typically ship within 3-5 working days. For made-to-order products, lead times range from 2-6 weeks depending on the order quantity, product complexity, and current production schedule. We provide estimated delivery timelines at the time of order confirmation and keep customers updated on any changes.

Do you export internationally?

Yes, we regularly export our products to various countries. We have experience with international shipping logistics, customs documentation, and compliance with import regulations in different markets. Our export team can guide you through the entire process and help arrange shipping according to your preferences.

What packaging options do you offer?

We offer multiple packaging options to suit different needs:

  • Standard carton boxes
  • Wooden cases for heavy shipments
  • Vacuum packing for corrosion-sensitive items
  • Customer-specific packaging requirements
  • Bulk packaging for large quantities
All packaging is designed to protect products during transit and storage.

Still have questions?

Our team is ready to help with any additional questions you may have about our products and services.
